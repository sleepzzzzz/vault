## Basics
### Devastation Evoker Rotation in Midnight Season 1
Devastation Evoker is a fairly simple and straightforward with its spell priority. Cooldowns are mostly used on cooldown, while building and spending **정수 / 정수 폭발**.

* **Build and Spend:** **파열** and **기염** spend **정수**, primary resource for Evokers.
* **살아있는 불꽃** and **하늘빛 일격** are the filler spells that generate **정수 폭발**, a proc that allows you to use these abilities without spending Essence.
* Spend Essence and Essence Burst, then use filler spells while waiting.
* **Use cooldowns frequently:** **용의 분노** and **깊은 숨결** (Scalecommander) are Devastation's main cooldown.
* Essence Burst is guaranteed from filler spells during this buff.
* Its duration can be extended by both **영원의 쇄도** and **불의 숨결** with **적개심**.
* Given that both spells also come with their own short cooldowns, consider what is available when entering Dragonrage and to press them on cooldown during it.
* **Always be casting:** Try to maintain high uptime on targets.
* Constant use of damaging spells is the key to high damage in Raid and Mythic+.
* Use **부양** effectively to cast spells on the move.
* Try to fill "downtime" in your rotation with spells, even if its not completely optimal (Azure Strike for instance).
* Using something is almost always better than being idle. Try to use cooldowns and Empowered spells early and often.

### Single-Target Priority
**Best Devastation Evoker Single Target Rotation**
Devastation in Single Target plays around a simple priority list. This means with each cast and GCD we go top to bottom on a list, and press the button with the current highest value.

With **Scalecommander** selected, the priority is as follows:
(The builds used below are covered in the Talents page.)

1. **부양** if capped and **깊은 숨결** is ready soon.
2. **깊은 숨결**.
3. **깊은 숨결** when **공중 포격** is about to expire.
4. **용의 분노**.
5. **전세역전**.
6. **영원의 쇄도** Rank 1.
7. **불의 숨결 Rank 1**.
8. Channel **파열** with **대규모 파열**.
9. Channel **파열**.
10. **하늘빛 휩쓸기**.
11. **살아있는 불꽃** as filler.
12. **하늘빛 일격** if needed for movement.

**Further Details**
* Scalecommander frequently uses **깊은 숨결** even in Single Target.
* In addition, each time you use **불의 숨결** and **영원의 쇄도**, you gain a buff that changes **파열** into **대규모 파열**.
* **부양** can be cast while casting, and allows you to cast while moving.
* With **후방기류**, a **부양** charge resets with each **깊은 숨결**.
* The **부양** buff grows in duration with each cast, so be sure to spend a **부양** charge before each **후방기류**.
* **깊은 숨결** is very strong and should be used on cooldown, but you don't want to stay in the animation longer than needed.
* Use the **깊은 숨결** cancel macro found on the macros page.
    * **Example Macro for 파열:**
      ```
      #showtooltip Disintegrate
      /cancelaura Deep Breath
      /cast Disintegrate
      ```
    * This macro will end your **깊은 숨결** with the first press and use **파열** with the second. You would tap it twice, right as you hit your target.

### AoE Priority
**Best Devastation Evoker AoE Rotation**
For Devastation, AoE is defined as 3 or more targets. Devastation plays mostly the same in AoE, aside from sometimes casting **기염**. Ranking Empowered spells up can also be beneficial.

With **Scalecommander** selected, the priority is as follows:

1. **부양** if capped and **깊은 숨결** is ready soon.
2. **깊은 숨결**.
3. **용의 분노**.
4. **전세역전** on **영원의 쇄도**.
5. **불의 숨결** Rank 1.
6. **영원의 쇄도** Rank 1.
7. **깊은 숨결** with **공중 포격**.
8. **기염** with >= 18 stacks of **충전 폭발** at 4+ targets.
9. Channel **파열** with **대규모 파열**.
10. **기염** at 4+ targets.
11. Otherwise, channel **파열**.
12. **하늘빛 휩쓸기**.
13. **살아있는 불꽃** with **화염 도약** and **불태움**.
14. **하늘빛 일격** as filler.

**Further Details**
* In AoE, **깊은 숨결** with **공중 포격** does not have a specific delay. It is simply lower priority than other spells since it is no longer holding up the **깊은 숨결** cooldown.
* Currently, it is not worth it to rank Empowers up in Single Target or AoE. Use **불의 숨결** and **영원의 쇄도** at Rank 1, unless you have **대규모 파열**.
* **충전 폭발** stacks are worth tracking. Try to avoid casting **기염** in the 5 or so seconds before **용의 분노** to gain 3x value on the **용의 분노 기염s**.
* Swap targets after each **대규모 파열** to apply **폭격** to multiple targets to get more value on **연장전**.

### Opener
**Best Devastation Evoker Opener**
The optimal Devastation Evoker opener sets up your **용의 분노** and uses various cooldowns, trinkets, or items. Following that, we transition into our main rotation.

2. Pre-cast **살아있는 불꽃**.

## Pre-Combat Checks
Before any combat happens, it is important for Devastation Evoker to make sure the following are done (Raids, dungeons, delves, or open-world).

* **Cast Group Buffs:** Use **청동용군단의 축복** to buff you and your allies.
* **Heal up:** Use **살아있는 불꽃 / 에메랄드 꽃 / 신록의 품** to heal yourself and allies. This will fill your **진홍빛 적응** buff, if it does not overheal.
* **Consider Threat (Optional):** Think twice about going full blast if Tanks are inexperienced. You have no way to drop aggro if you pull before Tanks establish threat.

## Devastation Evoker Major Cooldowns
Devastation Evoker has one main cooldown: **용의 분노**. Other cooldowns are minor, or group buffs.

### Dragonrage
**용의 분노** is your main cooldown. This buff guarantees **정수 폭발** procs from **살아있는 불꽃** and **하늘빛 일격** for the duration. Try to use it every 2 minutes.

* **Key Takeaways:**
    * Activating this spell fires 3 **기염s** at random targets.
    * Gain the full effect of **특화: 거물 사냥꾼** with **폭정**.
    * **불의 숨결** and **영원의 쇄도** extend the buff with **적개심**.
    * **용의 분노** can be held for AoE, but it is a fairly long buff.
    * Average duration: ~32 seconds with proper play.
    * Apex talents buff your damage during this cooldown (**분노 상승**).
    * Gain a damage buff after **용의 분노** expires with **분노 상승**.

### Empower Spells
**불의 숨결** and **영원의 쇄도** are Devastation's Empower spells.

* **불의 숨결:**
    * Empowering moves damage from the debuff into the initial hit.
    * **화염 도약** increases target count per level.
    * The DoT component scales dynamically with **특화: 거물 사냥꾼**.
* **영원의 쇄도:**
    * Empowering increases target count.
    * **영원의 전역** doubles target count per level.
* **Flameshaper:** Use at Rank 1 in ST and AoE (without **전세역전**). **화염 흡수** uses **불의 숨결** to fuel explosions.
* **Scalecommander:** Use at Rank 1 in ST and AoE. Each use enables **대규모 파열**, which marks targets for **폭격**.

### Tip The Scales
**전세역전** is a minor cooldown.
* Used in **용의 분노** to instantly cast **영원의 쇄도** or **불의 숨결** at max rank.
* **Flameshaper:** Use only on **영원의 쇄도**.
* **Scalecommander:** Can be used on either **영원의 쇄도** or **불의 숨결**.

### Deep Breath
**깊은 숨결** is a notable cooldown. Always gets full **특화: 거물 사냥꾼** value with **폭정**.
* Use at minimum range unless needed for movement.
* Ground effects can still hit you while flying.
* **Talent Modifiers:**
    * **임박한 파괴:** Decreases Essence costs & increases damage.
    * **공중 포격:** Increases damage & allows second use.
    * **오닉스 유산:** Reduces cooldown.
    * **시간 증폭:** Stretches damage taken for safety.
* **Scalecommander:** Uses on cooldown. Can be steered and ejected from. Cooldown reduced by **날개지도자**.
* **Flameshaper:** Typically not used for damage unless **임박한 파괴** is taken.

### Fury of the Aspects
**위상의 격노** is a group cooldown.
* Grants **피의 욕망** effect (Haste).
* Off the GCD.
* Applies **소진** debuff (10 minutes).

## Devastation Evoker Advanced Tips
The best thing to do is to **always be casting**. Idle time will greatly affect your damage.

### Hover and Movement
* **부양** is key to minimizing damage loss. Can be cast while casting.
* Subsequent casts add to duration.
* **활공:** Faster than running. Triple tap to hit the ground quicker. Can avoid some ground effects at the peak of the jump.
* **Quirk:** **부양** allows you to **활공** while channeling **파열**.
* **Scalecommander:** Easier time with **부양** due to **후방기류** refunds from **깊은 숨결**.

### Deep Breath Z-axis (Scalecommander)
* Preserves Z-axis while flying.
* Avoid using uphill (pebbles can stop you).
* Gain high ground or **활공** before using to fly over obstacles or ground mechanics.
* **Movement Combo (200 yards):** Jump **활공** -> **부양** x2 -> **깊은 숨결** -> **깊은 숨결** -> **부양** x2 (refunded) -> **시간의 와류** -> **부양** -> **활공**.

### Disintegrate Chaining and Clipping
* **파열** has 5 ticks (with **하늘빛 기민함**) or 4 ticks.
* **Chaining:** Cast again after the 4th tick. Remaining duration/tick carries over (new channel becomes 6 ticks). No value loss.
* **Clipping:** Cutting it short by interrupting or chaining too early. 
    * **Scalecommander:** Avoid chaining **대규모 파열** into normal **파열** (loss of buff percentage).
    * Clipping non-**대규모 파열** one tick early for Empowers or **깊은 숨결** is a tiny (0.4%) gain for Scalecommander, but risky.
