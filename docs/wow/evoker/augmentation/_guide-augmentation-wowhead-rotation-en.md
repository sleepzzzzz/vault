# [wowhead] Augmentation Evoker Rotation in Midnight Season 1

At its most basic, the bread and butter of Augmentation gameplay is all about weaving together your key damaging abilities and group buffs in order to maximize the damage output of your group.

## Key Concepts

Here are some of the big ideas to consider while playing Augmentation:

* **Keep your buffs up:** Augmentation's rotation is centered around maximizing uptime of **칠흑의 힘**.
* **시간의 모래:** Allows us to extend the duration of **Ebon Might** through our damaging rotation: casting **지각 변동** and **불의 숨결** at appropriate ranks on cooldown, spending resources on **분출**, and filling downtime with **살아있는 불꽃** (or **하늘빛 일격**).
* **Always be casting:** Keeping high uptime on your rotational spells is key to maintaining a solid resource economy in both raids and Mythic+.
* **Mobility:** Use **부양** effectively to cast spells on the move, and make sure to fill any "downtime" in your rotation with your filler spells, **Living Flame** and **Azure Strike**, in order to deal damage and generate **정수 폭발**.
* **Watch your Essence Burst:** Augmentation Evoker has a few different potential sources of **정수 폭발**, and the buff only stacks up to 2. Make sure to spend your **Essence Burst** when capped, ideally before casting **정수 폭발** generators like **예지** and **Living Flame** to avoid wasting resources.
* **Mind your cooldowns:** It's often correct to send cooldowns as soon as they've come back up, but look for opportunities to align your major cooldowns like **영겁의 숨결** with allies' cooldowns and big damage moments during an encounter.

## Best Augmentation Evoker Rotation

At its core, Augmentation Evoker follows a simple spell priority aimed at maximizing uptime of buffs on allies. Augmentation spends its resources while buffs are active to extend buff duration, and spends its downtime replenishing resources before starting the cycle over again.

### [Single-Target Priority]
**Best Augmentation Evoker Single Target Rotation**
Augmentation in Single Target plays around a spell priority list, while making sure to avoid spending resources outside of **칠흑의 힘** whenever possible, in order to maximize damage and uptime of buffs on allies.

**With Scalecommander selected, the priority is as follows:**
1.  Maintain **예지** on chosen DPS players.
2.  Cast **칠흑의 힘** if a few seconds or less remain on the active buff duration.
3.  Cast **영겁의 숨결** (see section on cooldown usage for more details).
4.  Cast **전세역전** (see section on cooldown usage for more details).
5.  Cast **불의 숨결** at Rank 1.
6.  Cast **지각 변동** at Rank 1, unless increased radius is needed to hit relevant targets.
7.  Cast **분출**.
8.  Cast **살아있는 불꽃** as filler in nearly all situations.
9.  Cast **하늘빛 일격** as backup filler that can be cast while moving, or used to slow enemies.
10. Maintain **끓어오르는 비늘** on a Tank.

### [AoE Priority]
**Best Augmentation Evoker AoE Rotation**
Augmentation essentially plays the same in AoE as it does in Single Target.

* Note that **지각 변동** can be cast at a higher rank to hit enemies in a wider area.
* When playing **Scalecommander**, do make sure to play around **폭격** whenever possible (covered in Advanced Tips).

**With Scalecommander selected, the priority is as follows:**
1.  Maintain **예지** on chosen DPS players.
2.  Cast **칠흑의 힘** if a few seconds or less remain on the active buff duration.
3.  Cast **영겁의 숨결** (see section on cooldown usage for more details).
4.  Cast **전세역전** (see section on cooldown usage for more details).
5.  Cast **불의 숨결** at Rank 1.
6.  Cast **지각 변동** at Rank 1, unless increased radius is needed to hit relevant targets.
7.  Cast **분출**.
8.  Cast **살아있는 불꽃** as filler in nearly all situations.
9.  Cast **하늘빛 일격** as backup filler that can be cast while moving, or used to slow enemies.

## [Opener]
**Best Augmentation Evoker Opener**
The recommended opener has changed slightly in Midnight: we now immediately cast **칠흑의 힘** after the start of combat to begin buffing the whole raid as soon as possible, and then proceed to cast **예지** and **영겁의 숨결**.

* Be sure to avoid pairing **전세역전** with **불의 숨결**.
* If we have **녹아내린 불씨** talented, we want to maximize uptime of the **불의 숨결** damage over time effect.

**Scalecommander Opener Sequence:**
1.  Pre-cast **끓어오르는 비늘** on a Tank.
2.  Pre-cast **살아있는 불꽃**.
3.  Cast **칠흑의 힘**.
4.  Cast **예지** on Buff Target 1. Use this time to position for **영겁의 숨결**.
5.  Cast **예지** on Buff Target 2.
6.  Cast **영겁의 숨결**. Aim for a short, safe flight path.
7.  Use trinket and **빛의 잠재력**.
8.  **불의 숨결** at Rank 1.
9.  Cast **전세역전** (off-GCD).
10. **지각 변동** at Max Rank from **전세역전**.
11. Cast **분출** until dry on Essence, and fill with **살아있는 불꽃** as needed.
12. Proceed onto the main rotation.

## [Pre-combat Checks]
Before any combat happens (raids, dungeons, delves, or open-world):

* **Check Consumables:** Food, flask, weapon oil, augment rune.
* **Cast your Raid Buff:** Use **청동용군단의 축복** (1-hour duration).
* **Apply Source of Magic:** Cast **마법의 원천** on your chosen healer (1-hour duration).
* **Apply Blistering Scales:** Cast **끓어오르는 비늘** on your chosen target (usually the tank).
* **(If talented) Draconic Attunements:** Check preferred **용족의 조율** (**검은 조율** is default).
* **(If talented) Apply Timelessness:** Cast **무궁** on your chosen target (high threat DPS).
* **(If talented) Cast Bestow Weyrnstone:** Cast **웨이른석 부여** on your chosen target.

## Augmentation Evoker Major Cooldowns

### Empower Spells
Ideally, ensure you only cast **불의 숨결** and **지각 변동** while **칠흑의 힘** is up to extend its duration and direct **흐르는 모래** towards allies.

* **불의 숨결:** Higher Ranks increase upfront damage but reduce DoT duration.
* **화염 도약:** Benefits from higher Ranks by increasing **살아있는 불꽃** projectiles (each can generate **정수 폭발**).
* **녹아내린 불씨 (Capstone):** Encourages maximizing DoT uptime. With this, always cast **불의 숨결** at **Rank 1**, and cast it before **지각 변동**.
* **지각 변동:** Empowering simply expands the radius. Rank 1 is sufficient for single-target. Use higher ranks in AoE to hit more targets.

### Tip The Scales
* **With 녹아내린 불씨:** Use **전세역전** on cooldown just before casting **지각 변동**. This keeps **불의 숨결** at Rank 1 while getting an instant Empower spell.
* **Without 녹아내린 불씨:** Pair **전세역전** with either spell depending on the need for speed or radius.
* **Note:** Using **전세역전** always saves time and allows casting while moving.

### Chronowarden Tip The Scales
Treat **시간의 폭발** from **전세역전** as a major DPS cooldown. Align it with **영겁의 숨결**. Even if **시간은혜** reduces the cooldown, it is generally best to continue pairing **전세역전** with **영겁의 숨결**.

### Ebon Might
**칠흑의 힘** is a baseline 30-second cooldown buff.
* When down, build resources and hold extension abilities for the next window.
* **Pandemic Mechanic:** You can re-cast while active to add remaining duration (up to 30% of base).
* **Optimal Timing:** Re-cast with **3-4 seconds remaining**.
* Main stat updates dynamically based on caster's Intellect.

### Breath of Eons
* **Ideal Usage:** Align the 10-second **Temporal Wounds** window with allies' highest-burst windows.
* **Practical Usage:** Cast roughly on cooldown to maximize uses.
* **Flexibility:** In Mythic Raids, be aware of specific timings like damage amplifications or add phases.

### Time Skip
If playing **시간 건너뛰기** instead of **엮인 실타래**:
* Channeling results in tremendous value when multiple strong abilities are on cooldown.
* **First Use:** Recommended during the opener after the first set of **불의 숨결** and **지각 변동**.
* **Subsequent Uses:** Essentially on cooldown, aligning with **시간의 폭발** (as Chronowarden) and ideally when it reduces cooldowns of **불의 숨결**, **지각 변동**, **예지**, and **영겁의 숨결** by the full 20 seconds.

## Augmentation Evoker Advanced Tips

### Bombardments
When playing **Scalecommander**:
* Empower spells grant a buff causing the next **분출** to be **대규모 분출** and apply **폭격**.
* This stacks up to 2.
* **Spreading Bombardments:** In 2+ target situations, cast the second **분출** on a different priority target to have **폭격** rolling on both.
* **연장전:** When activated, it extends all active **폭격**, increasing damage and further reducing **영겁의 숨결** cooldown via **날개지도자**.
