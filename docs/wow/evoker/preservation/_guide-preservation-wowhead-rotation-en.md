## Preservation Evoker Rotation in Midnight Prepatch
Preservation Evoker is an unconventional healer with a few unique mechanics that can make it a little more daunting to pick up, but it's also extremely fun once you do. Midnight removes quite a few buttons and interactions, which should greatly improve the accessibility of the spec.

Here are some core concepts to keep in mind, especially if you're new to the spec:

* **Use Short Cooldowns Regularly:** Cast 시간 변칙 the moment it comes off cooldown. It generates a lot of 메아리 buffs and heals for a decent amount via its absorb.
* **Cast 꿈의 숨결 regularly** when you do not have 메아리 buffs out.
* **Cast Echo:** Use your Essence resource and 정수 폭발s and try to avoid wasting any of either. You will mostly cast 메아리, but with the 쌍둥이 메아리 talent, you might weave with 에메랄드 꽃.
* **Consume Echoes:** Consume 메아리 buffs with powerful spells like 메리스라의 축복 (or 되감기) and 신록의 품.

## Best Preservation Evoker Healing Priority and Rotation
Both Preservation hero trees are extremely good so we'll look through the playstyle of each. In raid, both trees play very similarly with some minor differences in nuance and where your power is allocated. In Mythic+, the playstyles diverge a little more.

### Hero Talent Master Switches
Bear in mind, current recommendation in all content is **Chronowarden** though **Flameshaper** is also a powerful tree.

### Raid Healing
Preservation has a unique 메아리 mechanic where two of your spells (Echo and 시간 변칙) put 메아리 buffs on allies. The next spell you cast on any target will consume all 메아리 buffs you have out and duplicate whatever spell you cast to all of them.

* If you Echo target A, and then cast 신록의 품 on target B then both A and B will get a 신록의 품 heal and any associated effects.
* If you put five 메아리 buffs out with 시간 변칙 then press 되감기, then all five of them will get a 되감기 heal-over-time effect.

This mechanic gives us superb flexibility in our healing style since we can prepare for damage by putting out 메아리 buffs and then consume them with a range of different spells depending on the situation we're in.

#### Tell me more about Echo
Echo also copies all extra talents you have for the spell that you consume them with. If you 되감기, then they will all:
1. Heal for 15% of the damage they took recently (사활의 순간).
2. Receive 10% more healing from you in the future (은총의 시간).
3. Give you a 15% chance each of proccing 정수 폭발.

> **Note:** The only effect that does not 메아리 is 생명 결속 which is now limited to one target. 메아리 buffs also won't be consumed by 에메랄드 꽃 - only 살아있는 불꽃, 신록의 품, 되감기 and 꿈의 숨결 - your core healing spells.

You'll often put out quite a few 메아리 buffs at a time before consuming them. This is sometimes referred to as a **"Ramp"** - a build-up of buffs before a big payoff phase where you heal a lot of people at once with one of your best spells.

### Priority - Emphasize Short Cooldowns
There are two key components to preservation healing:
1. 메아리 and the spells you will consume them with.
2. Your short cooldown spells. Cast 시간 변칙 as often as possible, and 꿈의 숨결 just after consuming your 메아리 buffs. They are expensive but do more than enough healing to justify it.

#### Quick Chronowarden Priority List
* Cast **시간 변칙** on cooldown.
* Cast **꿈의 숨결** often. Cast it after you have consumed 메아리 buffs to avoid consuming them with 꿈의 숨결.
* Cast **신록의 품** within a 15-second window.
* Cast **에메랄드 꽃** if you have an 정수 폭발.
* Cast **메아리** if you have Essence available and either no 정수 폭발 buff or you are at two stacks of 쌍둥이 메아리.
* Consume **메아리 buffs** with 메리스라의 축복 (common) or 신록의 품 (rare).
* Cast **살아있는 불꽃** on the boss to farm 정수 폭발s and contribute a little damage. You get a large mana refund if you cast it on an enemy instead of an ally.

#### Echo vs Emerald Blossom
The 쌍둥이 메아리 talent incentivizes 에메랄드 꽃 usage by giving us a double 메아리 on our next cast if we do. 에메랄드 꽃 is also expensive, both in terms of mana and essence. However, 정수 폭발 removes both of those costs. If you have two bursts you can cast two 에메랄드 꽃 in a row and then your next two 메아리 casts will each cleave to a nearby ally.
* Therefore, we should always cast 에메랄드 꽃 when we have an 정수 폭발.
* It's sometimes ok to cast an 에메랄드 꽃 without one (mana permitting).
* It's acceptable to play without this talent if you'd prefer an easier playstyle.

#### When to consume Echo with which spell
There are two primary choices: **메리스라의 축복** and **신록의 품**.
* 메리스라의 축복 will be your primary choice but it is a proc so it won't always be available.
* Your core play pattern will revolve around farming 메리스라의 축복 (each 메아리 and 에메랄드 꽃 has a 20% chance to proc it) and then consuming your 메아리 buffs with it.
* If you get unlucky, it is ok to consume with 되감기 in a pinch.
* 신록의 품's niche is large healing absorbs since the amount it heals the primary 메아리 targets is higher than 메리스라의 축복.
* Remember that 메리스라의 축복 also puts a full strength 되감기 on the target with all associated effects!

#### I'm having mana trouble
* I don't recommend ever dropping 시간 변칙 casts because of how strong it is.
* Instead, drop filler casts of 살아있는 불꽃, or check your 에메랄드 꽃 usage to make sure you are not casting it without an 정수 폭발 buff.
* It's also reasonable to take the 마력의 순환 talent, which gives mana return on 파열 cast. If taken, you will use either Essence or 정수 폭발 on 파열 during downtime to recover.

### Mythic+ Healing
Preservation has a unique 메아리 mechanic where two of your spells (메아리 and 시간 변칙) put 메아리 buffs on allies. The next spell you cast on any party member will consume all 메아리 buffs you have out and duplicate whatever spell you cast to all of them.

* In Mythic+, we primarily use 메아리 by casting it on target A, then casting 신록의 품 on target B.
* 시간 변칙 is primarily used to put five 메아리 buffs out and then pressing 되감기 to give everyone a heal-over-time effect.

#### Cast Combo on Cooldown - 되감기 Uptime
This gives you a 100% uptime on the 되감기 buff on the entire group and a very frequent shield on everyone.
* **Why is this important?** 되감기 coverage puts a 10% healing buff on your entire group (은총의 시간), covers small incidental damage, and in Midnight will also give them a damage reduction effect. These will also give you a chance to proc 생명불꽃.

#### Heavier Damage - 신록의 품
When heavy damage is coming up you can use your 메아리 spell to put larger 메아리 buffs on your party.
1. Prioritize two DPS plus yourself.
2. Cast 신록의 품 on whoever you didn't 메아리.
3. The 신록의 품 cast is copied to your three 메아리 targets and all four of you will get a massive heal and a solid 8-second HoT.

#### Fitting in your other spells
* **꿈의 숨결:** Cast on cooldown at rank 1 if damage is low to moderate. Cast at rank 3 if you require a burst heal.
* **불의 숨결:** Massive AoE damage ability that'll also heal the party for a lot. Cast at rank 3.
* **시간 팽창:** Superb damage reduction for a single target. Low cooldown so use this regularly.
* **미풍:** Cast before AoE damage to reduce how much damage it does.

#### Spot Healing - Fixing Mistakes
If a single target takes a lot of damage:
* Cast 되감기 on the target immediately, or Echo -> 되감기 if you have an extra GCD.
* This takes advantage of 사활의 순간 which heals back a portion of damage taken.
* If they require more healing, use 신록의 품, 시간 팽창, or 살아있는 불꽃.

## Pre-Combat Checks
Before every raid or dungeon:
1. Cast **청동용군단의 축복** (raid buff) - every pull.
2. (Raid) Make sure **마법의 원천** is active on another healer.
3. Make sure you have a **Flask** on.
4. Make sure you have a **Food buff**.
5. Make sure you have **탈라시안 불사조 기름** on your weapon.
6. (Optional) Apply a **공허에 물든 증강의 룬**.
7. Cast **시간 변칙** a few seconds before you pull to start with 메아리 buffs.

## Preservation Evoker Major Cooldowns
### Stasis (정지장)
정지장 lets you store and then re-cast three spells. This rewards pre-emptive, planned use.
* Spells have no cost, no cooldown, and trigger in the order stored.
* Empowered spells cast at whatever Rank the original was cast at.
* Doesn't work on major cooldowns like 꿈의 비행 or 되돌리기.

#### Stasis Combos
* **Chronowarden Raid Combo:** 꿈의 숨결 (before Stasis) -> 정지장 -> 시간 변칙 (Rank 1) -> 메리스라의 축복 -> 시간 변칙 -> Release.
* **Flameshaper Combo:** 꿈의 숨결 (Rank X) -> 에메랄드 꽃 -> 에메랄드 꽃 -> Release.
* **Pre-pull Cheese:** Store three 시간 변칙 before the pull and release right before a damage event.
* **Mass Dispel:** Store three 소작의 불길 to clear the entire group.

### Dream Flight (꿈의 비행)
A large area AoE heal that sends you 10-50 yards, healing everyone beneath you and adding a lengthy HoT. It also applies a large absorb to you while in the air.

### Rewind (되돌리기)
Applies a 5 second HoT based on 33% of damage taken in the 5 seconds prior. It is doubled in dungeon content. Line this up with the most dangerous moments.

## Overcoming a 30 yard Range
Preservation is the only healer with a reduced 30 yard range (56% of standard area).
* Stand centrally whenever possible.
* Use mobility buttons (부양, 신록의 품, 구출, 미풍) often.
* Use 부양 as your go-to.
* If a target is about to run out of range, put a HoT (꿈의 숨결, 되감기) or an 메아리 on them.
* In Mythic+, ask group members to stay a little closer for 꿈의 숨결.
